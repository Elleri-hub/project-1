Landmarks

The colliseum is one of the oldest landmarks in Italy. It was commissioned as a gift to the people in 72 AD by Emperor Vespasian

The Leaning Tower of Pisa The Leaning Tower of Pisa is a medieval bell tower in Italy, famously known for its 4-degree tilt caused by inadequate foundations on soft ground, beginning during its construction in 1173

History

Italy was officially born as a unified nation on March 17, 1861

The Roman Empire was born in 27 BC in Rome, Italy, following the end of the Roman Republic and the Roman empire went on to be one of the strongest empires in history
