Ways to get to Italy!

You can get to Italy by train, plane, boat, or even bus!

Remember to have a valid visa and passport to travel there or the Italian police may arrest you.

Ways to get around Italy!

Italy has a very good public transportation system so you can buy a transportation pass and you'll have access to almost the entire city!

If you prefer to travel in your own vehicle there are many places at the airport and around the airport where you can rent a vehicle!

Airports

These are the major airports of Italy: Rome-Fiumicino (FCO), Milan-Malpensa (MXP), and Rome-Ciampino G.B. Pastine International Airport (CIA)
